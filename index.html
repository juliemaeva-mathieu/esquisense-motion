Monitoring Equestrian Performance

Equisense Motion allows you to track and analyze your horse's performance during training sessions and competitions. Gain valuable insights on speed, distance, jumps, and much more.

Symmetry and Locomotion Analysis

Thanks to Equisense Motion, monitor your horse's symmetry and locomotion to quickly detect potential health issues or lameness. Improve your horse's health and well-being by identifying early warning signs.

Personalized Training Program

The Equisense Motion app provides you with a personalized training program tailored to your horse. Follow the suggested exercises and tips to achieve your goals and enhance your horse's equestrian skills.
